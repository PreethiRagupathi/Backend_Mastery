Sea Animals

Angelfish are colorful reef fish.
They live in warm tropical waters.

Blue whales are the largest animals.
They can grow over 30 meters long.

Clownfish live among sea anemones.
They are famous from the movie Nemo.

Crabs have hard protective shells.
They walk sideways along the shore.

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals.
They communicate using clicks and whistles.

Jellyfish drift with ocean currents.
Some species can deliver painful stings.

Octopuses have eight flexible arms.
They can change color to hide.

Seahorses swim upright in the sea.
Males carry and protect the babies.

Sea turtles travel long distances.
They return to beaches to nest.

Starfish are marine invertebrates.
They can regrow lost arms.

Swordfish have long pointed bills.
They are among the fastest sea fish.

Walruses have long ivory tusks.
They live in cold Arctic waters.
